Data transformation and normalization

In the previous SENDA databases, the censoring time was set at the date of database withdrawal (November 13, 2019)., with their last treatment discharge on 2019-11-13. For the databases from 2019 to 2023, however, the date of discharge of the last database was 2023-04-28. The total amount of records in databases from 2010-2022 were 222,945 of 106,534 patients (distinct HASHs, or encrypted National ID). 60.8% (n= 20,812) were new to the SENDA Agreement 1.
